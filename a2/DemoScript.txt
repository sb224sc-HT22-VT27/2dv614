Set the scene:
A video producer is out on a studio set shooting high-res video clips on his iPhone. An editor is sitting at his desktop workstation across the room, waiting to start cutting the footage. 

The Problem: 
The file is 5.2 GB. AirDrop does not work because they are on different systems, and uploading it to Google Drive or WeTransfer would take quite some time just to compress and upload, and then double that for the editor to download.

*****START VIDEO******
